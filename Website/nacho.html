Nacho Tumbleweed Jr.

Nacho Tumbleweed Jr. is Rad Shiba's boss from the Canine Independent Rescue Association.

Appearance

Nacho is a Shiba Inu who wears a dark black shirt and an eye patch over his left eye.

Personality

Nacho has a gruff and no-nonsense attitude, but has a tendency to be just as playful and persistent as other dogs. He believes very much in providing for those under the care of CIRA, making sure that all dogs are treated equally and farily. Nacho also can sometimes be depressive, in which he explains to Jill in the ANNA Demo, was how he got his eyepatch.

Dana Zane also respects Nacho quite a lot, insisting that Jill gives him a drink even when the bar is about to close, and goes for a night of poker with Nacho and Rad Shiba. Nacho likes to think of life as a battlefield, and makes sure all his orders play out similarly to a mission.

Background

At some point before the events of the game, Nacho founded the Canine Independent Rescue Association (CIRA for short).

He graduated top of his class at hand-to-paw combat due to being the only one who watched the instruction video.
