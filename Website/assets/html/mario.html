Mario

Mario is a delivery man who initially visits VA-11 Hall-A acting like a tough guy, but then changes when Jill heavily chastises him for doing such a thing for respect.

Appearance

He has a relatively unkempt haircut and attempts to appear as a stereotypical tough biker, sporting typical biker attire, sticking to a red biker jacket with goggles strapped to his left arm. He has either a scar or a stylistic shaved portion cut out of his right eyebrow.

Personality

At his first appearance, he tries to put up a tough front, acting rude to Jill in order to keep up that front, but his true personality is actually much different. After he is confronted on his behavior by Jill, he acts more like himself, and he changes his typical drink orders to be more girly, and even starts to ask Jill if Gillian is single before becoming scared of revealing his orientation and leaving the bar.

In his first appearance he also comments that the boss is cute and "...not that you're not cute. And it's not like I'm into girls either," implying he is gay. At his second appearance he says clearly that he prefers men.

Mario's relationship with girly, cute things, his desire to be like "other bikers" hint that he is gay. He confirms this by hitting on Gillian.

Plot

Mario first comes into the bar on Day 9 (December 21) asking for the bathroom in a rude manner. He orders manly drinks to pretend before Jill, but secretly wants girly drinks. He says that he's a rebel and a motorcyclist but he actually works delivering mail for a courier company.
